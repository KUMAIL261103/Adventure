ADVENTURE TIME


Adventure tourism is one of the fastest growing segments, and that’s for food reason. In the past few years, popularity for adventure sports has grown significantly in urban areas, and people are looking at more than just fun. In fact, adventure sports have several benefits other than giving you an adrenaline rush. Here are some major ones:

Each human being has certain phobias within them, be it known or unknown. Some of the most common phobias included that of height, water, falling, etc. Taking part in adventure sports is a great option to tackle and overcome these fears. Activities like Swoop Swing, Flying Fox, and Sky Cycling are apt for beating acrophobia. Other adventure sports like Rappelling, High Rope Challenges, Water Zorbing, etc help in overcoming various other fears.

BIKING

Motocross can be a non-stop, full-body, high-heart rate workout, as well as an exercise in muscle strength and aerobic endurance as busting through soil and ruts alone can knock you around and tire you out.

Just like cycling, motocross can help you build a base, and strengthen your aerobic energy system. Motocross riders are some of the most physically fit and mentally tough athletes on the planet. Core strength, flexibility, mobility, recovery and repetition are all essential in Motocross, making it a sport ripe for crossover training and direct skill transfer to other sports if not overall fitness level.

Imagine how it feels to sense the air breeze on your face, how it feels to see the uninterrupted 360-degree astonishing views of the landscape around you as the birds do. Imagine yourself dancing through the skies as you experience human flight in its purest form

Apart from being an extremely fun and unforgettable experience which gives you the chance to see the world through different eyes. Furthermore, it gives you the opportunity of making great new friendships or experiencing the incomparable feeling of flying. But, besides that, you will have the chance to experience a wide range of positive both physical and mental health benefits of paragliding

SURFING

Surfing originated in Hawaii. These days, people surf all over the world – wherever there is a wave. Australia has almost 40,000 kilometres of coastline, with many surf beaches. Surfers enjoy the physical benefits of paddling and surfing, being in the fresh air, as well as the overall benefits of surfing.

Surfing provides many health benefits including: cardiovascular fitness – from paddling shoulder and back strength – these muscles will strengthen from the paddling leg and core strength – once you’re standing up on the board, strong legs and a strong core will keep you up
